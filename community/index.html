Join Our Community!

For more than a decade old PC game that barely runs anymore, Pekka Kana 2 has a surprisingly large and active fan community. Our Discord server currently has over 60 members (although not all are active). The fanbase used to be centered around the official Piste Gamez forum, but as internet forums have slowly become overthrown by social media, it is only natural that the community has moved from the forums to nowadays very popular Discord. Note that the forum is nonetheless still very much in use.

To join our Discord community, follow the following public invitation link:

discord.gg/KErBEvQ
